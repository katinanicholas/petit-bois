Community Highlights · July 2026

What Is Happening Near You

Red, White & Biloxi: An America250 Celebration

July 4, 5 to 9:30 p.m. at Biloxi Town Green, 710 Beach Blvd. A family friendly celebration honoring the nation's heritage with music, history, entertainment, and fireworks. Patriotic music, a dramatic presentation at 7 p.m., Unfazed Show & Band at 9 p.m., vendors, food trucks, and fireworks. Free admission.
biloxi.ms.us · Details: 228-435-6339

Biloxi Shuckers Independence Day Fireworks Extravaganza

July 4 at Keesler Federal Park. Gates open at 5:30 p.m. All military personnel receive discounted admission. Enjoy an exciting game followed by a post-game fireworks show that lights up the night sky.
biloxishuckers.com

Boom Boom Committee Fireworks Show

July 4 beginning at 9 p.m. Fireworks launched from a barge near Deer Island set to patriotic music broadcast on BOB 105.9. Best viewing spots range from the Biloxi Lighthouse to the Biloxi Bay Bridge. A fireworks-free zone is also available for spectators.
biloxi.ms.us

Stars, Stripes & Seafood: An America250 Celebration

July 3, 5:30 to 7:30 p.m. at the Maritime & Seafood Industry Museum, 115 1st St. Enjoy re-enactments from the Five Founding Fathers of the Seafood Industry. Refreshments served. Free admission.
maritimemuseum.org · Details: 228-435-6254

Donal M. Snyder Sr. Community Center

Activities for all ages including Senior Bingo and Ice Cream Social events throughout the month. The community center on 2520 Pass Road remains one of the most visited spots near Petit Bois with a four-lane lap pool, indoor walking track, and fitness room.
biloxi.ms.us · Details: 228-388-7170
